Tell us a bit about yourself.

My name is Wineke (Winnie) Engelage and I am originally from the Netherlands. I began to facilitate energy healing when I became a certified Reiki Master in 2007. Since then I’ve taken many different courses across the country. Today I reside in Markham Ontario and live with my husband Joe and my pup, Oliver Twist.

Can anyone become an energy healer?

Yes. Currently we are experiencing a global shift in consciousness. All of us have the ability to tap into different dimensions. We just have to be open to it.

A lot of people don’t know what energy healing is, or that it exists. When did you discover this type of healing?

When I was a young adult I became very ill. I was told I would never be able to work again. My intuition guided me to energy healing doctors and other dimensional facilitators.

Would you recommend energy healing over western medicine?

No. As amazing as energy healing is, it should not be replaced by modern medicine. It should go hand in hand with it.

What is your goal?

I used to be afraid people would judge me if I said certain things. My goal is to let go of this fear and be completely open and honest about healing. By doing this I hope to guide people through a shift in consciousness.
